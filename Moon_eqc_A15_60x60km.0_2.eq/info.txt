LRO Narrow Angle Camera, Mosaic Apollo 15

Abstract :This is a Lunar Reconnaisance Orbiter (LRO) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) mosaic centered at the Apollo 15 landing site with an extent of 60x60km. Each NAC image was processed in ISIS3 at a resolution of 50cm pixel, color balanced, and synthesized into a single output.
这是一个月球侦察轨道飞行器（LRO）窄角相机（NAC）马赛克，以阿波罗15号着陆点为中心，范围为60x60km。每个NAC图像在ISIS3中以50cm像素的分辨率进行处理，颜色平衡，并合成为单个输出。
