Title: Apollo 15 Metric Cam Image Mosaic
Description:
Abstract:
The Apollo 15 image mosaic is stored as 8-bit signed integer at 7.4m/pixel (4096ppd) resolution. The invalid pixels are marked with 0 value. Value 255 is left unused. The mosaicking process includes an upsampling post processing step. The mosaic is generated using the Ames Stereo Pipeline: a software tool produced by the NASA Ames Intelligent Robotics Group (IRG) for automated processing of stereo imagery.
Purpose:
This Digital Image Mosaic provides a high resolution representation of the equatorial regions of the Moon.

Process_Step:
Process_Description:
The Apollo 15 Digital Image Mosaic is computed from nadir looking Apollo 15 images used for the computation of the Digital Elevation model. The original images are ortho-projected on the corresponding DEM and processed to correct variations in exposure time. The range of Apollo images used here include all nadir looking images of the Apollo 15 Metric zone: AS15-M-0072.cub-AS-15-M-0103.cub, AS-15-M-0136.cub-AS-15-M-0160.cub, AS15-M-0281.cub-AS15-M-1426.cub, AS15-M-0460.cub-AS15-M-0602.cub, AS15-M-0872.cub-AS15-M-1031.cub, AS15-M-1052.cub-AS15-M-1159.cub, AS15-M-1562.cub-AS15-M-2491.cub.
