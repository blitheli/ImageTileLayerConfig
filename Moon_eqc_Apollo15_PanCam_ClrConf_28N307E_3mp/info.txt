Abstract :This is a colorized confidence map associated with the Digital Elevation Model (DEM) extracted from the Apollo 15 Panometric camera images for the Aristarchus Plateau 2 site. The DEM is controlled to the Lunar Orbiter Laser Altimeter (LOLA) dataset. DEM values referred to a radius of 1737400 meters. In the confidence data, the values are derived from precision values. For the colorized confidence, the value range from 10 to 14 was used, ranging from poor confidence (10) to very good confidence (14). This range was mapped to a color map varying from yellow to green. Manually interpolated values (15) are mapped to blue. All other values are mapped to fully transparent pixels.
