Abstract :This raster dataset represents a confidence value or figure of merit (FOM) for each pixel for the digital elevation model (DEM)for the Aristarchus Plateau Area 1. Resolution of the original Apollo 15 Panametric images was 1.5m/pixel which allows an output DEM resolution of 5m/pixel using a softcopy photogrammetry system. It is register to ULCN 2005 control network. The DEM was registered horizontally and vertically to LRO&apos;s Lunar Orbiter Laser Altimeter (LOLA) data. Elevation values are in meters and referred to a radius of 1737400m.
该光栅数据集表示阿里斯塔克斯高原1区数字高程模型（DEM）的每个像素的置信值或优值（FOM）。阿波罗15号原始巴拿马图像的分辨率为1.5米/像素，这允许使用软拷贝摄影测量系统输出5米/像素的DEM分辨率。它注册到ULCN 2005控制网络。DEM被水平地和垂直地登记到LRO&apos；月球轨道飞行器激光高度表（LOLA）数据。高程值以米为单位，指的是1737400m的半径。
