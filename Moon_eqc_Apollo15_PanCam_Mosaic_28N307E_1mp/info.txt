Abstract : This is a visible image mosaic for the Aristarchus Plateau Area 2. Resolution of the original Apollo 15 Panametric images was 1.5m/pixel. It is register to ULCN 2005 control network. The Mosaic was then registered horizontally and vertically to LRO's Lunar Orbiter Laser Altimeter (LOLA) data.
