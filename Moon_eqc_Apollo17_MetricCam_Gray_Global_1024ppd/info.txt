Abstract :The Apollo 17 DEM grayscale is generated from the Apollo 17 DEM data, by scaling elevation values from the range of -9135 to +8104 meters to integer values between 0 and 255. Elevation values below and above -9135 and 8104 meters are mapped to 0 and 255, respectively.
