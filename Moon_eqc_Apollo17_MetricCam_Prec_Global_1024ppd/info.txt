                            deviation of all the available Apollo 17 DEM files. The DEM precision
                            units are stored as 16-bit signed integer at 30mm/pixel resolution. The
                            DEM units are represented in meters. These DEM precision files were
                            generated using the Ames Stereo Pipeline: a software tool produced by
                            the NASA Ames Intelligent Robotics Group (IRG) for automated processing
                            of stereo imagery.
