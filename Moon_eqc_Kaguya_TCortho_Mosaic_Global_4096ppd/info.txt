Abstract :This mosaic represents the &quot;Kaguya&quot; Terrain Camera (TC) &quot;Ortho&quot; mosaic. Please note this mosaic was created using a different control network from other LMMP products (which are controlled to LOLA) and thus may not always register well to those prodcuts. The mosaic is derived from data acquired by the Terrain Camera (TC) instrument aboard the Selenological and Engineering Explorer (SELENE) satellite flown by the Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA). The mosaic can be considered a merged best-of version which was produced at 4096 pixels/degree (7.4 meter spatial resolution) and includes images from the low-sun Morning (early or right-to-left low-angle solar illumination) and Evening (late or left-to-right solar illumination) TC mosaics. The &#147;Ortho&#148; version is termed by the Kaguya team as indicating simulated vertical illumination.
