Abstract :This data product contains  elemental abundances values for Ti derived from LP gamma ray spectrometer [Feldman et al., 1999] observations acquired during the high- and low-altitude portions of the LP mission. This data is given in units of elemental weight percent. A description of the reduction of these data products is given by Prettyman et al. [2002]. The map bin size is 60 km by 60 km.
