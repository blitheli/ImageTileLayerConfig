Abstract :This is a shaded-relief of the original LOLA Digital Elevation Model (DEM). The DEM is a shape map (radius) of the Moon at a resolution of 256 pixels per degree, based on altimetry data acquired through Spetember 2011 by the LOLA instrument. The ground tracks were interpolated using the Generic Mapping Tools programs &quot;surface&quot; and &quot;grdblend&quot;. The map is in the form of a binary table with one row for each 0.0039065 degree of latitude, pixel registered. Map values are referred to a radius of 1737400 m.
