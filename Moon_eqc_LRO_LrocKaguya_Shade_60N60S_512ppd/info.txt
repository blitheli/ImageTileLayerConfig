Abstract :This is a shaded-relief of the original LOLA and Kaguya Terrain Camera Stereo Merged Digital Elevation Model (DEM) released in early 2015. The DEM is a shape map of the Moon at a resolution of 512 pixels per degree (59m/pixels), based on altimetry data acquired by the LOLA instrument and Kaguya Terrain Camera Stereo data. Map values are referred to a radius of 1737400 m. SELENE is a Japanese mission developed and operated by JAXA. Please create NASA&apos;s LOLA Team and JAXA&apos;s SELENE/Kaguya Team.
