Abstract :This is an 8bit grayscale represenation of the original CPR product stretched from 0 to 1.0. This mosaic is a Circular Polarization Ratio (CPR) product defined as the ratio of the same sense circular polarization to the opposite sense circular polarization (CPR = SC / OS). This Level 3 product is called a Derived Data Record (DDR) generated from multiple Level 2 CDRs. The average CPR (dimensionless) value of the entire lunar surface is on the order of 0.3. CPR values for certain types of surface deposits may be either anomalously low (near zero for very smooth terrain) or anomalously high (&gt;1.0 for blocky largescale rough features). High CPR also is due to volumetric backscattering from thick deposits of frozen volatiles, such as water-ice.
