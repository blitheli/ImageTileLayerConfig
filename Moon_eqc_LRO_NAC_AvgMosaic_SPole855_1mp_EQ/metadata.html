Title: LRO LROC NAC Image Mosaic, S Pole, Avg Merge
Description:
Abstract:
This is a visible image mosaic generated using Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) images from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter mission. The images were orthorectified using LRO's Lunar Orbiter Laser Altimeter (LOLA) polar 5 meter/pixel DEM. The mosaic is generated at half the resolution at which the individual LROC NAC images were gathered, which is approximately 0.5 meters/pixel. The mosaic was registered horizontally to the LOLA global DEM.
