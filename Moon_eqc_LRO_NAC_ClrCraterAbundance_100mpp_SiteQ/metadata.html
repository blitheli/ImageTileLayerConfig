Abstract:
This is a colorized crater abundance map based on the crater detections from the associated LRO NAC Crater Detections 0.83mpp, Endurance-A Site Q layer. Detected crater center points were plotted, diameter values were summarized within 100m by 100m area, points were then rasterized and output at a resolution of 100 m/px. Pixel values were normalized to represent the percentage of area covered by craters within each 100m bounding area.
