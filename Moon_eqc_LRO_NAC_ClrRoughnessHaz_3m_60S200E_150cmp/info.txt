Abstract :This is a thematic color roughness hazard map derived from the local roughness map LRO_NAC_Roughness_3m_60S200E_150cmp.tif of the South Pole-Aitken Basin Interior site. Roughness hazard classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the roughness scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 3m.
