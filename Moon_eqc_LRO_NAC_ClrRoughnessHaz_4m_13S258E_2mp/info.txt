Abstract :This is a thematic color roughness hazard map derived from the local roughness map LRO_NAC_Roughness_4m_13S358E_200cmp.tif of Alphonsus Crater. Roughness hazard classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the roughness scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 4m.
