Abstract :This is a thematic color roughness hazard map derived from the local roughness map LRO_NAC_Roughness_15m_25N311E_2mp.tif of Gruithuisen Domes. Roughness hazard classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the roughness scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 15m.
