Abstract :This is a thematic color roughness hazard map derived from the local roughness map LRO_NAC_Roughness_8m_20S337E_400cmp.tif of Bullialdus Crater. Roughness classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the roughness scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 8m.
