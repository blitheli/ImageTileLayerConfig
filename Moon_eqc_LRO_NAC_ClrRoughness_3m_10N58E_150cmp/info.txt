Abstract :This is a thematic color roughness map derived from the local roughness map LRO_NAC_Roughness_3m_10N058E_150cmp.tif of Mare Crisium. Roughness classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the roughness scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 3m.
