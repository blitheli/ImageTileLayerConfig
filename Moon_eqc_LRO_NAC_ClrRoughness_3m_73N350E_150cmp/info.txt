Abstract :This is a thematic color roughness map derived from the local roughness map LRO_NAC_Roughness_3m_73N350E_150cmp.tif of Anaxagoras Crater. Roughness classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the roughness scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 3m.
