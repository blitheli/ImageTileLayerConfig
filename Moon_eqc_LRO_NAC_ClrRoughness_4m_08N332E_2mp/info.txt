Abstract :This is a thematic color roughness map derived from the local roughness map LRO_NAC_Roughness_4m_08N332E_2mp.tif of Hortensius Domes. Roughness classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the roughness scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 4m.
