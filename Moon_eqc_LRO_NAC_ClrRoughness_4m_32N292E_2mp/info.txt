Abstract :This is a thematic color roughness map derived from the local roughness map LRO_NAC_Roughness_4m_32N292E_2mp.tif of Lichtenberg Crater. Roughness classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the roughness scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 4m.
