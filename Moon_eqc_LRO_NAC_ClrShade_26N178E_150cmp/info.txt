Abstract :This is a colorized shaded relief of the digital elevation model (DEM) extracted from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) images from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter mission. The data product is a DEM from all NAC images that were acquired and made available via PDS at the time of the DEM construction. Images included are from both the Commisioning and the Mapping mission phases and vary from approaximately 2.m/p to 0.5 m/p in resolution at the image boresight. The typical DEM scale is 1.5 m/pixel with a comparable horizontal resolution. The DEM was registered horizontally and vertically to LRO&apos;s Lunar Orbiter Laser Altimeter (LOLA) data. Elevation values are in meters and referred to a radius of 1737400m.
