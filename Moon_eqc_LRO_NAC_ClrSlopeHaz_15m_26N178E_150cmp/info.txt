Abstract :This is a thematic color slope hazard map derived from the local slope map LRO_NAC_Slope_15m_26N178E_150cmp.tif of Dante Crater. Slope hazard classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the slope scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 15m.
