Abstract :This is a thematic color slope hazard map derived from the local slope map LRO_NAC_Slope_3m_26N004E_150cmp.tif of Apollo 15. Slope hazard classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the slope scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 3m.
