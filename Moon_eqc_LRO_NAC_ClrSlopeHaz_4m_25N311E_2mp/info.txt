Abstract :This is a thematic color slope hazard map derived from the local slope map LRO_NAC_Slope_4m_25N311E_2mp.tif of Aristarchus 1. Slope hazard classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the slope scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 4m.
