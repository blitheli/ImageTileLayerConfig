Abstract :This is a thematic color slope map derived from the local slope map LRO_NAC_Slope_15m_02S167E_150cmp.tif of the Stratton (Dewar) site. Slope classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the slope scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 15m.
