Abstract :This is a thematic color slope map derived from the local slope map LRO_NAC_Slope_15m_36N320E_2mp.tif of Gruithuisen Domes. Slope classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the slope scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 15m.
