Abstract :This is a thematic color slope map derived from the local slope map LRO_NAC_Slope_3m_10N058E_150cmp.tif of Mare Crisium. Slope classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the slope scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 3m.
