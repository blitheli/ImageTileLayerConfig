Abstract :This is a thematic color slope map derived from the local slope map LRO_NAC_Slope_4m_08N332E_2mp.tif of Hortensius Domes. Slope classification color ranges are arbitrary but representative of recent lander and rover designs, where the slope scale is equivalent to the vehicle diameter of 4m.
