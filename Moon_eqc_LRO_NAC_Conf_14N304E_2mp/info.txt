Abstract :This raster dataset represents a confidence value or figure of merit (FOM) for each pixel for the digital elevation model (DEM) as extracted from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) stereo images. The original data product is a DEM from stereo images acquired at approximately 0.5 m/p resolution, and the DEM is set at 2m/p, downsampled to minimize noise. This DEM confidence map covers the Marius Hills site.
