Abstract :This raster dataset represents a confidence value or figure of merit (FOM) for each pixel for the digital elevation model (DEM) as extracted from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) stereo images. The original DEM product is generated from stereo images acquired at approximately 0.5m/p resolution, which allows an output DEM resolution of 1.5 to 2 meters/pixel using a softcopy photogrammetry system. However, in some cases, LROC DEMs were also generated at lower resolutions. The DEM was registered horizontally and vertically to LRO&apos;s Lunar Orbiter Laser Altimeter (LOLA) data.
该光栅数据集表示从月球勘测轨道飞行器相机（LROC）窄角相机（NAC）立体图像中提取的数字高程模型（DEM）的每个像素的置信值或优值（FOM）。原始DEM产品由以约0.5米/p分辨率采集的立体图像生成，这允许使用软拷贝摄影测量系统输出1.5至2米/像素的DEM分辨率。然而，在某些情况下，LROC DEM也以较低的分辨率生成。DEM被水平地和垂直地登记到LRO&apos；月球轨道飞行器激光高度表（LOLA）数据。
