Abstract :This is a confidence value map for each pixel of the digital elevation model (DEM) extracted from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) images from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter mission. The data product is a DEM from all NAC images that were acquired and made available via PDS at the time of the DEM construction. Images used are from both the Commisioning and the Mapping mission phases and vary from approximately 2.0 m/p to 0.5 m/p in resolution at the image boresight. The DEM scale is 1.5 m/pixel with a comparable true horizontal resolution. The DEM was registered horizontally and vertically to LRO&apos;s Lunar Orbiter Laser Altimeter (LOLA) data via use of the LOLA NAIF kernels. Subsequently it was compared to the LOLA gridded DTMs. Elevation values are in meters and referred to a radius of 1737400m.
