Abstract :This is a crater abundance mosaic of the South Pole-Aitken Basin Interior site generated from data files containing the percentage of area per hectare (100m x 100m) that is occupied by detected craters in the Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) Images covering this site.  The data files are created from crater lists computed from images acquired by the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter (LRO) Camera (LROC) NAC. Craters are modeled as ellipses. Craters of 2.5m on the major axis or larger can be fully resolved from the images.  The smallest craters detected are approximately 2.0m on the major axis. Overlapping craters and craters inside craters are reported individually. The spatial distribution of craters is given in three different maps for each NAC image: a crater abundance data map, a crater abundance color (thematic) map, and a crater abundance hazard color (thematic) map. These are available for download as part of the product LRO LROC Hazards, South Pole Aitken Basin Interior, All. See also the crater density data products for each site.
