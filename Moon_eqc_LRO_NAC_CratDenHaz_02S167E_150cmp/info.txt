Abstract :This is a crater density hazard mosaic of the Stratton (Dewar) High Fe Anomaly site generated from data files containing the number of detected craters (all sizes are included) per hectare (100m x 100m) in the Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) Images covering this site.  The data files are created from crater lists computed from images acquired by the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter (LRO) Camera (LROC) NAC. Craters are modeled as ellipses. Craters of 2.5m on the major axis or larger can be fully resolved from the images. The smallest craters detected are approximately 2.0m on the major axis. Overlapping craters and craters inside craters are reported individually. The spatial distribution of craters is given in three different maps for each NAC image: a crater density data map, a crater density color (thematic) map, and a crater density hazard color (thematic) map. These are available for download as part of the product LRO LROC Hazards, Stratton (Dewar) High Fe Anomaly, All. See also the crater abundance data products for each site.
