Abstract :This is a grayscale image version of the Digital Elevation Model (DEM) extracted from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) geometric stereo images from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter mission. This image was generated from the matching Stratton (Dewar) DEM data by scaling the elevation values from the range of 234 and 1564 to integer values between 0 and 255. Elevation below and above the 234 and 1564 are mapped to 0 and 255, respectively.
这是数字高程模型（DEM）的灰度图像版本，从月球勘测轨道飞行器任务的月球勘测轨道器相机（LROC）窄角相机（NAC）几何立体图像中提取。该图像是通过将234至1564之间的高程值缩放为0至255之间的整数值，从匹配的斯特拉顿（杜瓦）DEM数据生成的。234和1564下方和上方的高程分别映射为0和255。
