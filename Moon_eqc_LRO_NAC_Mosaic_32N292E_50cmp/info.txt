Abstract :This is an orthophoto mosaic of the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) stereo images used to create a digital elevation model (DEM) of the same area. Resolution of the original LROC NAC images and output mosaic was approximately 0.5m/pixel. The DEM was registered horizontally and vertically to the LRO Lunar Orbiter Laser Altimeter (LOLA) data. DEM values are in meters and referred to a radius of 1737400 meters. This image mosaic covers the Lichtenberg Crater site.
