Abstract :This is a rock abundance hazard mosaic of the Tycho Crater site generated from data files containing the percentage of area per hectare (100m x 100m) that is occupied by detected rocks in the Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) Images covering this site. The data files are created from rock lists computed from images acquired by the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter (LRO) Camera (LROC) NAC. Rocks of 2.5m diameter or larger can be fully resolved from the images.  The smallest rocks detected are approximately 1.0m in diameter. Clumped rocks may be reported as a single large rock when their shadows are merged.  In addition to this mosaic, the spatial distribution of rocks is given in three different maps for each NAC image: a rock abundance data map, a rock abundance color (thematic) map, and a rock abundance hazard color (thematic) map. These are available for download as part of the product LRO LROC Hazards, Tycho Crater, All. See also the rock density data products for each site.
