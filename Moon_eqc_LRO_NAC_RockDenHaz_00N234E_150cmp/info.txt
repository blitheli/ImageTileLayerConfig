Abstract :This is a rock density hazard mosaic of the Hertzsprung site generated from data files containing the number of detected rocks (all sizes are included) per hectare (100m x 100m) in the Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) Images covering this site. The data files are created from rock lists computed from images acquired by the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter (LRO) Camera (LROC) NAC. Rocks of 2.5m diameter or larger can be fully resolved from the images.  The smallest rocks detected are approximately 1.0m in diameter. Clumped rocks may be reported as a single large rock when their shadows are merged.  In addition to this mosaic, the spatial distribution of rocks is given in three different maps for each NAC image: a rock density data map, a rock density color (thematic) map, and a rock density hazard color (thematic) map. These are available for download as part of the product LRO LROC Hazards, Hertzsprung, All. See also the rock abundance data products for each site.
