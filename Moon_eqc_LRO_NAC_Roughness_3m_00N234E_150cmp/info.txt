Abstract :This is a local roughness map derived from the Digital Elevation Model (DEM) LRO_NAC_DEM_00N234E_150cmp.tif of Hertzsprung, with a post spacing of 1.5 and a roughness baseline (scale) of 3m. Pixel values are in meters and the theoretical roughness uncertainty equivalent to that of the source DEM. See also the corresponding local roughness and roughness hazard color (thematic) maps.
