Abstract :This is a shaded-relief of the digital elevation model (DEM) extracted from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) stereo images from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter mission. The original data product is a DEM from stereo images acquired at approximately 0.5m/p resolution, which allows an output DEM resolution of 1.5 to 2 meters/pixel using a softcopy photogrammetry system. However, in some cases, LROC DEMs were also generated at lower resolutions. The DEM was registered horizontally and vertically to LRO&apos;s Lunar Orbiter Laser Altimeter (LOLA) data.
