Abstract :This is a colorized shaded-relief slope map of the Digital Elevation Model (DEM) extracted from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) geometric stereo images from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter mission. The original data product is a DEM from stereo images acquired at approximately 0.5 m/p resolution, and the DEM is set at 2m/p, downsampled to minimize noise. The DEM covers the Lichtenberg Crater site. The DEM is controlled to the Lunar Orbiter Laser Altimeter (LOLA) dataset. DEM values are in meters and referred to a radius of 1737400 meters.
