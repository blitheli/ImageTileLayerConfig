Data_Quality_Information:
Logical_Consistency_Report:
These images were projected on to the global LOLA DTM and using default spacecraft ephemeris from USGS ISIS, without any further alignment or warping.
Completeness_Report:
The orthoimage covers a 20 km box centered on Picard, with one exception: an square with around 0.2 deg sides centered on 54.99, 13.45 in which the only available NAC image (M132467815RE) was completely black. Pixels in this area have nodata values.
