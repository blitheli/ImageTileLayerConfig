Abstract :This is a preliminary global mosaic of the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Wide Angle Camera (WAC) images taken in monochrome mode. The global WAC mosaic provides a global morphology basemap with incidence angles between 60 deg and 80 deg and a uniform resolution of 100 m/pixel.
