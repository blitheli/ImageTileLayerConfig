Description:
Abstract:
This is a preliminary global mosaic of the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) Wide Angle Camera (WAC) images taken in monochrome mode. The global WAC mosaic provides a global morphology basemap with incidence angles between 60 deg and 80 deg and a uniform resolution of 100 m/pixel.
Purpose:
The LROC WAC global mosaic provides an improved morphologic map of the Moon by acquiring images at sun angles optimal for morphological mapping.

Process_Step:
Process_Description:
To even out the incidence angle variations, the equatorial mosaic is comprised of data collected over three periods (1/20/2010 to 1/28/2010, 5/30/2010 to 6/6/2010, 7/24/2010 to 7/31/2010). The South Pole images were acquired 8/10/10 to 9/19/10 and the North Pole images were acquired 4/22/10 to 5/19/10. Some gores were filled with data taken at other times. The non-polar images were map projected onto the GLD100 (WAC derived 100 m/pixel DTM), while polar images were map projected on the LOLA shape model (80 deg to 90 deg; N/S) and the GLD100 (60 deg to 80 deg N/S). In addition, the LOLA derived crossover corrected ephemeris and an improved camera pointing provide accurate positioning of each WAC image.
