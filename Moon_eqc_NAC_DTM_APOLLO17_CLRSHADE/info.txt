LRO Narrow Angle Camera, ColorHillShade Apollo 17

Abstract :Color-coded topography draped over the shaded relief from a 5m Digital Terrain Model (DTM) of the Apollo 17 landing site created with Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) stereo image pairs from the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter Camera (LROC) aboard the Lunar Reconnaissance Orbiter (LRO). The shaded relief image was generated with the sun azimuth of 315 degrees (northwest) and sun altitude of 45 degrees.
彩色编码地形覆盖在阿波罗17号着陆场5米数字地形模型（DTM）的阴影上，该模型由月球勘测轨道飞行器（LRO）上的月球勘测轨道器相机（LROC）的窄角相机（NAC）立体图像对创建。生成的阴影浮雕图像的太阳方位角为315度（西北），太阳高度为45度。
